NewsPicks is already a great app – 4.6 stars and 2K reviews on the App Store – and I have a few ideas to take it to the next level.

First project: basic UX Cleanup

Nothing crazy here, just some more spacing, cleaner UI, and basic grammar edits.

Flesh out and improve these topics, create hierarchy

Show most popular picks on top of picker profile

Make current article more prominent and sticky

Feature build: creating compelling NewsPicks Recommendations.

Good recommendations can improve retention and engagement while avoiding the "filter bubble" problem.

We can help users find content they might like (not necessarily agree with) based on their positive and negative picks, as well as the picks of their favorite pickers.

Big product build: something new called "NewsPicks Discussions"

One of the goals of NewsPicks is to encourage healthy discussion and debate around important topics. Here's what that would look like:

  • A platform (i.e. high total value) for people to argue about and discuss news
  • A system to encourage positivity and discourage and remove "toxic" comments
  • Seamless integration with chat to encourage personal connection

Most of the work will be properly designing incentives and UX to make sure debate is productive.

Big product build: the NewsPicks API

NewsPicks is uniquely positioned (both data and product-wise) to create an authoritative API for detecting fake news, provided as a service to publishers and platforms.

The NewsPicks API helps expand reach: encouraging healthy debate on other platforms too.
